Who We Are

SEMCO (Semirara Multi-Purpose Cooperative) is a member-owned financial institution established in 1989. We serve the community of Semirara Island by providing essential financial services and fostering economic growth through cooperative principles.

Our Mission

To provide quality financial services and empower our members through sustainable cooperative solutions that enhance their economic and social well-being.

Our Vision

To be the leading cooperative in fostering economic growth and community development in Semirara Island, recognized for excellence in member service and financial stability.
