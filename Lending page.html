Lending Services

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

Fast Approval Secure Process Competitive Rates

Loan proceeds are usually intended for investment/business, house repair, housing payment, hospital/medical/maternity charges, tuition, and other school fees, occasions/events expenses, etc.

Our Loan Services

Long Term Loan

Extended financing solutions for major investments:

  • Requirements: 2 latest payslips, Open for restructure
  • For ₱200k loan: Minimum 15 co-makers
  • Member's Qualification:
    • • Regular Member (Payroll) - 10+ years
    • • Regular Member (Payroll) - below 10 years
    • • Associate Member (Payroll) - below 5 years
    • • Regular & Associate (Non-payroll)
  • Payment Terms: 1yr / 2yr / 3yrs
  • Interest Rates: 17% / 24% / 33%

Short Term Loan

Quick financial solutions for immediate needs:

  • Requirements: 2 latest payslips, Open for restructure
  • Member's Qualification: All SEMCO Members
  • Payment Terms: 3 months / 6 months
  • Interest Rates: 25% (3 mos) / 10% (6 mos)

Appliances Loan

Finance your home appliance purchases:

  • Requirements: 2 latest payslips, Not allowed for restructure
  • Member's Qualification:
    • • All Regular and Associate Members (Payroll)
    • • Non-Payroll deduction members
  • Payment Terms: 3 months / 6 months / 1 year
  • Interest Rates:
    • • 10% (3 months)
    • • 15% (6 months)
    • • 22% (1 year)

Educational Loan

Support for educational expenses:

  • Requirements:
    • • Photocopy of Birth Certificate of Beneficiary
    • • School records (ID, Enrollment Record, etc.)
    • • Official Receipt
    • • Open for restructure
  • Member's Qualification:
    • • All Members with children, siblings studying in any school level
  • Payment Terms: 6 months / 1 year / 2 years
  • Interest Rates:
    • • 8% (6 months)
    • • 15% (1 year)
    • • 22% (2 years)
